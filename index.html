WELCOME TO PRESIDENTIAL BUSINESS SCHOOL

Presidential Business School (PBS) is an academic institution founded in 2011 AD, with the objective of establishing itself as a model center for quality education in Nepal. PBS is affiliated to Westcliff University, USA. Westcliff University is accredited by the WASC Senior College and University Commission (WSCUC), a regional accreditation body recognized by the U.S. Department of Education (ED), the Council on Higher Education Accreditation (CHEA).
